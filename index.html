About Nakne Studio

Founded in 2018, Nakne Studio is a team of passionate developers, artists, and storytellers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of mobile horror gaming. We believe in creating games that are not only terrifying but also visually stunning and technically impressive.

Our approach combines cutting-edge mobile optimization techniques with artistic vision, ensuring that our games run smoothly on a wide range of devices without compromising on atmosphere or visual fidelity.
